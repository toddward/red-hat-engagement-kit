# Customer Kickoff Checklist

Preparation checklist for the customer kickoff meeting.

## Pre-Kickoff

- [ ] Customer points of contact identified
- [ ] Attendee list confirmed from both sides
- [ ] Kickoff agenda drafted and shared
- [ ] Engagement overview deck prepared
- [ ] Meeting logistics confirmed (room, AV, remote access)

## During Kickoff

- [ ] Introductions and role clarifications completed
- [ ] Engagement scope and objectives reviewed
- [ ] Timeline and milestones walked through
- [ ] Communication and escalation paths agreed
- [ ] Data and access requirements discussed
- [ ] Success criteria aligned

## Post-Kickoff

- [ ] Meeting notes distributed
- [ ] Action items assigned and tracked
- [ ] Follow-up meetings scheduled
- [ ] Access requests initiated from kickoff discussion
- [ ] Engagement repository shared with customer (if applicable)
