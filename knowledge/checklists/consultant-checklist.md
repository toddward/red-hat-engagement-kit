# Consultant Checklist

Pre-engagement preparation checklist for consultants.

## Technical Readiness

- [ ] Lab environment provisioned and tested
- [ ] Demo scenarios prepared and validated
- [ ] Customer-specific configurations documented
- [ ] Runbooks for common procedures ready

## Knowledge Transfer

- [ ] Briefed on customer's industry and business context
- [ ] Familiar with customer's current technology stack
- [ ] Reviewed engagement scope and objectives
- [ ] Understood deliverable expectations

## Materials

- [ ] Workshop materials prepared (if applicable)
- [ ] Hands-on exercises tested in lab
- [ ] Reference documentation bookmarked
- [ ] Troubleshooting guides available

## Coordination

- [ ] Daily sync schedule with architect confirmed
- [ ] Aware of customer working hours and holidays
- [ ] Emergency contact list available
- [ ] Status reporting cadence understood
